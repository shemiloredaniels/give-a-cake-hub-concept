How it works

From your kitchen to someone's table

Three simple steps to connect your baking with neighbors who need it.

01

Select your state

Choose your state from the map below. We'll show you all the volunteer bakers and nonprofit partners in your area.

02

Enter your zip code

Narrow results to your neighborhood to find the closest bakers and drop-off partners within driving distance.

03

Claim a donation slot

Pick an open date on the rolling 3-month calendar and commit to baking for a specific nonprofit on that day.

04

Bake & deliver

Show up on your date, drop off your baked goods, and know that someone's day just got a little sweeter.
